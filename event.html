Rules


    1. Debate-Hindi

  • 2 from each House, one for the motion & the other against the motion.
  • Each member will be given 4 minutes to speak and 1 minute to refute. The refutation shall begin with the last speaker. 1st warning Buzzer will be after 3 minutes.
  • Issue for the Debate will be decided by the coordinators of the event.
  • Voice Modulation (including suitable use of accents), content, suitability and propriety of language.
  • Matter–the logical reasoning and content presented by the debater.
  • Manner–presentation and delivery, the style of speaking, body language.
  • Method–structure and organization of the presentation made by the speakers.

    • 2. Debate-English

  • 2 from each House, one for the motion & the other against the motion.
  • Each member will be given 4 minutes to speak and 1 minute to refute. The refutation shall begin with the last speaker. 1st warning Buzzer will be after 3 minutes.
  • Issue for the Debate will be decided by the Faculty Coordinators of the event.
  • Voice Modulation (including suitable use of accents), content, suitability and propriety of language.
  • Matter–the logical reasoning and content presented by the debater.
  • Manner–presentation and delivery, the style of speaking and body language.
  • Method–structure and organization of the presentation made by the speakers.

    • 3. Poetry–Hindi/English/Urdu

  • 3 (1 for English, 1 for Hindi and 1 for Urdu) from each House.
  • 3-5 minutes for each recitation.
  • Topic will be given only two hours before the event and the participant is required to write poetry in a place specified by the event coordinators.
  • Medium of poetry is English, Hindi and Urdu.
  • The participant will have to submit the content of the poem in writing to the judges after the recitation.
  • Judging Criteria: Choice of words, diction, creativity, and style of recitation, relevance to the theme.

    • 4. Essay Writing–Hindi & English

  • 2 (individual) from each House.
  • 2 hours.
    1. The topic will be announced on the spot.
    2. The essay should reflect participant’s point of view.
    3. The essay should be between 300-500 words.
    4. The language of writing is English/Hindi.
  • Judging criteria:
    1. Language
    2. Articulation
    3. Argumentation and Justification.
    4. Counter arguments and their criticism.
    5. A viable conclusion.

    • 5. Quiz

  • 3 from each house.
  • The Quiz will be based on the following topics:
    Science, Geog., History & Culture, Sports, Current Affairs
  • The Specific rules regarding evaluation procedure, time to reply a particular answer and the type of round will be given before the actual start of the competition.

    • 6. Creative Writing

  • 2 from each House.
  • 3 hours.
    1. The situation/theme and first line of the story will be announced on     the spot.
    2. The participants are required to make a well-formed, original story.     The situation/theme may belong to any time, any era, any place, and     any culture. The level of expression must appropriate the character     of the situation/theme.
    3. The story should be of 600 – 1200 words.
    4. The language of writing can be English/Hindi.
  • Judging criteria:
    1. Ability to captivate the reader till the end
    2. Originality and articulation.

    GENERAL INSTRUCTIONS:

    • 1. Light Indian Vocal

  • Team Size: 1 participants from each house + 5 accompanists (max).
  • Dress Code: Contestants are asked to wear appropriate attire to the competition.
  • 3-5 minutes for performance + 3 minutes for set up and clearance.
  • Instruments Provided: 2 Guitars, 1 Bass, 1 Drum Kit, 1 Synthesizer and 1 Tabla-set
    1. The participants are required to come prepared with a song of their     choice to showcase their versatility.
    2. Ghazal/bhajan/shabad/Sufi and Abhangas can be presented.
    3. Karaoke not allowed.
    4. Accompanists from other houses are allowed.
  • Judging Criteria: Judgment is on the qualities like Swara, Taal, Innovation, Synchronization, Selection of Raga, Composition and Overall Impression.

    • 2. Sangam

  • Team Size: 5-8 including the accompanist.
  • 6-10 minutes (including setup and clearance time).
  • A team member can sing and play an instrument as well. However such a person would be counted as both vocalist and accompanist.
  • Only Piano mode in key board is allowed, touch sense is allowed in keyboard.
  • Time Limit should be strictly followed. Participants will be penalized by judges for not doing so.
  • The decision of the organizers with respect to line-up would be final.
  • The decision of the judges will be binding.
  • The team may sing more than one song within the time limit.
  • Karaoke not allowed.
  • Judging Criteria: Content, Delivery and quality of Vocals, Presentation, Synchronization, Stage presence and overall performance as a group.

    • 3. Western Vocal

  • 1-2 from each House +5 accompanists (max).
  • Dress Code: Contestants are asked to wear appropriate attire to the competition.
  • 3-5 minutes for performance + 3 minutes for set up and clearance.
  • Instruments Provided: 2 Guitars, 1 Bass Guitar, 1 Drum Kit and 1 Synthesizer.
    1. Only Western songs with no classical tinge are allowed.
    2. Genres can be anything ranging from Rock to Rap and from Death Metal     to Soft Singing.
    3. Language of the song can be anything excluding those of Indian     Subcontinents.
    4. Karaoke not allowed.
    5. Accompanists from other houses are allowed.
  • Judging Criteria: Judgment is made on the qualities like rhythm, vocals, composition coordination and overall impression.

    • 4. Hindi Duet

  • 2 from each house + 5 accompanists (max).
  • Dress Code: Contestants are asked to wear appropriate attire to the competition.
  • 5 minutes for performance + 2 minutes for set up and clearance.
  • Instruments Provided: 2 Guitars, 1 Bass, 1 Drum Kit, 1 Synthesizer and 1 Tabla-set.
    1. The participants have to come prepared with a song of their choice to     showcase their versatility.
    2. The songs must have Hindi lyrics.
    3. Karaoke not allowed.
    4. Accompanists from other houses are allowed.
  • Judging Criteria: Synchronization, Presentation, Rhythm, delivery and quality of vocals and overall impact.

    • 5. Instrumental

  • 2-10 participants from each house.
  • 5-8 minutes for performance + 2 minutes for set up and clearance.
  • Instruments provided: As per requirements.
    (Participants may bring their own instruments but must inform to the music coordinators 2 days prior to the event).
    1. The participants have to come prepared with an instrumental song         (fusion is allowed).
    2. In the use of any electronic keyboard, no pre-programmed music is     allowed.
    3. The contestant must create all sound live on stage.
    4. Non-traditional instruments can be used.
    5. Accompanists from other houses are not allowed.
  • Judging Criteria: Melody, synchronization, audience response, stage presentation, creativity.

    • 6. Qawwali

  • Main singers: 1-2
    Accompanists: 4-6
  • 5-8 minutes (3 minutes set up and clearance time).
  • Dress Code: Salwar Suit/Kurta-Pyjama (optional), Topi and Jacket (optional).
  • Instruments Provided: Harmonium, Tabla and Synthesizer,
    1. The participants are required to perform a prepared Qawwali, which is a     form of Sufi devotional music. The performance has to be primarily in     Hindi (although light usage of Urdu is acceptable).
    2. Usage of any other instrument should be notified to the coordinators     and has to be arranged by the University itself.
    3. If any background music or beat is used, it would lead to negative     marking or even disqualification.
    4. No professional accompanists are allowed.
  • Judging Criteria: Content, Delivery and quality of Vocals, Presentation, Synchronization,and overall impact.

    • 1. Street Play

  • Upto 12 participants from each House.
  • Time Limit: 10-15 minutes.
    1. Medium of the performance is Hindi.
    2. The play should contain a social message.
    3. Musical instruments using electric power are not allowed. Any other     Musical instruments, including unconventional ones are allowed and will     be considered as props.
    4. No pre-recorded music or voice is allowed.
    5. No microphones will be provided.
  • Judging Criteria: Creative interpretation of theme and Impact of theme, Group co-ordination, Presentation, Contact with & involvement of the Audience and Overall impact.

    • 2. Skit

  • Team Size: Upto 8 participants.
  • 10-12 minutes (includes setup and clearance time).
    1. Each presentation should have a title and a theme.
    2. Medium of performing the Skit should be Hindi.
    3. The Skit can involve multiple scenes and blackouts.
    4. Backstage voice overs or sounds or narration are allowed during the     Performance.
    5. Background or Pre-recorded music is allowed.
  • Production material provided: 1 table, 2 chairs.
    Any other production material has to be brought by the teams but the Organizers reserve the right to allow its use on the stage.
  • Judging Criteria: Presentation of the theme, Group co-ordination, Dialogue Delivery and Overall impact, Acting, Make-up and Costumes according to the Act.

    • 3. Stage Drama

  • Team size: Upto 15 (participants + Accompanists).
  • Time Limit: 25-35 minutes (including stage setup and clearance time).
    1. Live music and backstage voices are allowed.
    2. The play should be in Hindi language whereas small portions of other     languages may be used.
    3. If using background music it should be made sure that there are no     dialogues present in the music or negative marks accordingly will be     rewarded.
    4.Recorded sounds are not allowed during the Performance.
  • Production material provided: 1 table, 2 chairs.
    Any other production material has to be brought by the teams but the Organizers reserve the right to allow its use on the stage.
  • Judging Criteria: Music, expressions, body language, overall impact, make-up and costumes according to the Act, stage setup, and synchronization with dialogues.

    • 4. Stand Up Comedy(Stand alone)

  • Team Size: Max 1 participants (individual) from each house.
  • Time limit: 5-10 min will be given to each individual.
    1. As according with the title participants have to entertain humoursly.
    2. Music between the acts is not allowed.
  • Judging Criteria: Humor, Body Language, Presentation, etc.

    • 5. Mime

  • Team Size: Upto 10 participants(max)
  • Time limit: 5-10 minutes (includes setup and clearance time).
    1. The act should not contain any dialogues, lip sync or props.
    2. No act shall contain any offensive, obscene, disrespectful actions or     gestures. The act will be immediately stopped and the entry will be     disqualified, if these instructions are not followed
    3. Only recorded music can be used, during the enactment, no lyrics     please.
    4. The act should be able to convey a message.
  • Judging Criteria: Points will be given on the basis of innovation, depiction of the situation,team work and expressions.

    GENERAL INSTRUCTIONS:

    • 1. Spot the Hidden Talent

  • Team Size: 3 individuals from each house.
  • Style : Folk
  • Time Limit: Ist round: maximum 90 seconds
    IInd round: maximum 90 seconds
  • General Rules:
    1. Participation is open to Fresher belonging to the respective house.
    2. Each House must report at 5:45pm.
    3. Once Event Starts participant will not be allowed to discuss anything     with anyone except the Participants.
    4. Violation of the above rules will lead to disqualification of House from     the event.
    5. Top 5 Participants will qualify for Round II.
  • Judging Criteria: Entertainment.

    • 2. Fashion Show

  • Team Size: Unlimited as per required.
  • Time limit: Running time for each video (5-12min.): Maximum Fifteen (15) minutes, including any titles and/or credits. Entries longer than Fifteen (15) minutes will lead to negative marking.
  • General Rules:
    Each house has to prepare main design using dark colors according to the theme. Material may include products made from paper, plastics, rubber, etc. Use of Paper is allowed for making dresses but fabric should be used as the base and length of dress should be up to knee length and protective layers should be used as per order by the Dean of students.

  • Judging Criteria:
    1. Use of the colors.
    2. Original and creative approach.
    3. Overall Effect.
  • Theme:
    Will be discussed with the committee and all the house coordinator.

    • 3. Mea Culpa

  • Team Size: 3 members team from each house, Including 1 prosecutor (M/F).
  • Time limit: 5-8 minutes for each defender.
  • Topic:
    Characters will be assigned to both the members (1M+1F) of a House 3 days before the event through a raffle (chit system).The prosecutor will coordinate the proceeding enacting the role of a trial taker.Every House will pose only one question to each defender of the other House. For this purpose, a House will prepare 8 allegations, 2 each for the defenders of every other House. Moreover, a House is required to submit the names of four students, one for every other House, who will ask the questions. In the last, the prosecutor can entertain at most one open question from the audience. Judges can interfere to discard any questions from the audience, if necessary.
  • Judging Criteria:
    Defender:
    1. Argumentative value of the defence.
    2. Voice Modulation (including suitable use of accents), content,     suitability and propriety of language.
    Prosecutor:
    1. Effective coordination of the proceeding.
    2. Presence of Mind
    3. Critical Attitude
